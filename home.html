About

Gabe Fletcher is a college student originally from Macon, GA with a passion for audio visual arts attending Shenandoah University in Winchester, VA as a Sound Design major. Gabe has been very active in the performing arts space acting as a sound designer or engineer for 8 high school productions over 4 years. You can find Gabe on Instagram and GitHub.
