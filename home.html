About

AUDIO ENGINEERING

Gabe Fletcher, originally from musically-rich Macon, GA , is now based in Winchester, VA where he is part of Shenandoah Conservatory at Shenandoah University. He has mixed and produced audio for multiple professional events, including musical performances, live broadcasts, interactive audio experiences, and more.
